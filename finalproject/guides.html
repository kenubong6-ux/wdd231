Ultimate Windows 11 Sim Racing Optimization

Published by Ubong Ken | Difficulty: Beginner

1. Enable Game Mode

Windows Game Mode prevents Windows Update from performing driver installations and sending restart notifications. It also helps achieve a more stable frame rate.

  • Press the Windows Key and type "Game Mode".
  • Select Game Mode settings.
  • Toggle the switch to On.

2. Disable Xbox Game Bar (If not recording)

If you use OBS or Nvidia Shadowplay to record your races, the Xbox Game Bar is just eating up background memory.

  • Go to Settings > Gaming > Xbox Game Bar.
  • Toggle it to Off.

3. High Performance Power Plan

Don't let your PC bottleneck your CPU just to save a few watts of electricity.

  • Open Control Panel > Hardware and Sound > Power Options.
  • Click "Show additional plans" and select High Performance.
